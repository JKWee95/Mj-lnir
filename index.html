Mjölnir
Mjollnir, Old Norse Mjöllnir, the hammer of the thunder god, Thor, and the symbol of his power.
Forged by the dwarven brothers Brokkr and Eitri, Mjölnir is the most feared weapon in the Nine Realms, capable of leveling entire mountains with only one hit
A symbol of both fertility and destruction, and was thought to possess regenerative healing powers
